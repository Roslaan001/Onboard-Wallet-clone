Frequently Asked Questions

What is Onboard?

Onboard is the simplest self-custody wallet to use for everyone. If you're looking for a wallet where you can completely own your assets and take control of your finances without relying on any middlemen, Onboard is the right choice for you


What is a self custody wallet?

A self-custody wallet is a digital wallet that lets you securely store your cryptocurrencies, digital assets, and tokens while keeping them private. Unlike centralised exchanges, where you give custody of your assets to a third-party platform, a self-custody wallet lets you control your assets because you manage them yourself. Some examples of self-custody wallets are Trust Wallet, MetaMask, and Onboard. On the other hand, when you use a centralised exchange like Coinbase or Binance, you use a wallet that is controlled by a third party. This means you have less control over your assets than you would with a self-custody wallet.


Can I use Onboard Wallet anywhere?

Yes, you can use the Onboard Wallet anywhere.


What local currencies and payment methods does Onboard support?

Currently, Onboard supports the Nigerian currency (Naira), Kenyan shillings (KES) and two payment methods: bank transfers and mobile wallets. Additional currencies and payment methods will be added in the coming weeks.


What digital assets can I swap for my local currency?

Onboard currently lets you swap Binance Smart Chain tokens BNB, USDT, BUSD, and USDC to your local currency. We are currently working on allowing you swap to your local currency from any popular token in the coming weeks.


Why do I need to verify my identity to swap assets to my local currency and how long does verification take?

To ensure a safe and secure platform, we verify the identity of every user. The verification process typically takes less than 24 hours. We will contact you if there are any issues with your verification. If you experience significant delays with your verification, please contact us at support@onboard.xyz for assistance.


How does Onboard protect my assets?

We help secure your wallet through private key management and multi-factor authentication.


Are there transaction limits on Onboard Wallet?

You can move digital assets from your Onboard wallet to another wallet without transaction limits. But when exchanging from digital assets to local currencies, there are limits:

  • Maximum amount for a single transaction: 2,000 USD


  • Maximum amount per day: 6,000 USD


What happens if something goes wrong with my transaction?

If you encounter a problem with your transaction, you can report it by clicking on the "report" button. This will create a dispute chat that includes you, the counterparty, and an Onboard customer support agent. Please provide any evidence you have to support your concern with the transaction, so that the dispute can be resolved as quickly as possible.
